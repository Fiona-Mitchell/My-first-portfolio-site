A little about me

Designer interested in combining fashion with tech.

My design studies have developed my critical and analytical skills, with the ability to think outside the box to solve complex issues - creatively. In May 2018 I graduated with a BA hons in Design (1st) from The University of Edinburgh (exchange to the Amsterdam Institute of Fashion). I have been fortunate enough to live and work around the world, which has provided me with an abundance of knowledge and experiences that I have channeled towards my character and work ethic. Whilst completing my studies in Amsterdam I joined the ethical luxury brand OSIER as an accessories designer, and since assisted with marketing strategies, international sales, and sustainability events; bringing OSIER from a two-man team to an award-winning brand retailing in global markets. Throughout my education and work experience I have confidently developed skills in research, CAD, prototyping; including programs such as Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Lectra Modaris, and Sketch Up. I enjoy challenging myself and collaborating with others by initiating relevant innovative design with practical feasibility, whilst always looking for a unique point of difference.


Since graduation I have delved deeper into digital design, programming and coding. I am currently learning React.js, with certifications in HTML, CSS, Javascript & Python.
